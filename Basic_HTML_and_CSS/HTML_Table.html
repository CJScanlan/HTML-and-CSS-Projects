Top 10 Breweries in the World

According to RateBeer (2019), notes pulled from vinepair.com
Rank Name Location Notes
1 Hill Farmstead Brewery Greensboro, VT. Has been in top 10 for the past 7 years - and only opened in 2010.
2 Side Project Brewing Maplewood, MO. Literally started as a side project.
3 Trillium Brewing Company Boston, MASS. Founded 2013 - major influence in New England IPA style. Cut wages in taproom from $8 to $5 in 2018 (yikes)- but since has reversed.
4 Tree House Brewing Company Charlton, MASS. Major influencer in New England IPA style
5 Cigar City Brewing Tampa, FLA. Named best craft brewery in Florida by VinePair
6 Alesmith Brewing Company San Diego, CA Includes a museum dedicated to hometown Padres.
7 Founders Brewing Grand Rapids, MICH. Damaged reputation from racial discrimination lawsuit and allegations of homophobia, but still in the top.
8 Cloudwater Brew CO. Manchester, England Only non-US brewery in the top 10.
9 Sante Adairius Rustic Ales Capitola, CA Known for oak-aged sour beers
10 FireStone Walker Brewing Paso Robles, CA I can personally confirm their beer is good
