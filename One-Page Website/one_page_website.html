Macramé by Carly

About

Beginning as a simple curiosity, I took up the hobby of hand-weaving macramé. I already had an appreciation of cool rocks and sticks and all that nature produces, so it was easy to create a collection of unique driftwood. From there on, I began to learn the process of tying simple knots, increasing with complexity over time. Weaving macramé has become quite therapeutic, providing my mind with an easy break and a canvas of creativity.
Certain pieces include artifacts of past adventures I've taken, as well as unique stones that tell a story.
Each piece is thoughtfully woven with 100% cotton rope and driftwood collected from various beaches along the Californian coast.
